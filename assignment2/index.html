About Me

I have been active in the field of training and technology education for the past ten years. I have taught both in the classrooms as well as teaching teachers how to weave technology into the curriculum and incorporate smart devices in the classroom.

I have been a board member of numerous school and community groups and headed fundraising and community outreach programs. In previous lives, I have worked as an assistant director for high school and community play productions, administrative/office manager, Computer/Media Specialist, teacher and market analyst in the oil tanker industry.
